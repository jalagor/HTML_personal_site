John thinks you should give these albums a listen

A Subjective List

In the Aeroplane over the Sea - Neutral Milk Hotel

A great indie album, mixing brass, acoustic guitars and excellent lyrics.

To Pimp a Butterfly - Kendrick Lamar

Perhaps my favorite rap album. The best moment is perhaps the transition from A1 to How Much a Dollar Really Cost.

9 Dead Alive - Rodrigo y Gabriela

Spanish guitar is fantastic and if you haven't heard any, start here.

Discovery - Daft Punk

Daft Punk was my first introdcution to EDM, and this album was the gateway.

Jungle - Jungle

smooth. cool. funky. Bust out the audiophile setup.

Omnium Gatherum - King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard

This band has so much music, and it hits tons of genres. They put on one of the best performances I've ever seen.
